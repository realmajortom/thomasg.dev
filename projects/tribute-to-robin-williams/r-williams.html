Robin Williams

"I am the master of my own fate, I am the captain of my soul."

I've written and rewritten my "tribute" Robin Williams for days now. It feels borderline sacrilegous to cram the expanse of his accomplishments and the depth of his legacy into an modest, beginner coding project. He's just too big for this kind of thing. Instead of word-dumping a benign wall of generic bio-vomit, I'll share some thoughts of my own.

The first performance I saw of Robin was his role as Peter Pan in Hook. Probably every other Sunday afternoon from the ages of 6 to 12, I'd pop the movie in the VCR and watch him bangarang! If only I could go to Neverland and have a food fight with Pan. Williams brought that world to life. For this, he will always be figurepiece of my childhood.

  • Good Will Hunting
