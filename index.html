    I'm Thomas!

    After much time spent shuffling between career paths and passions, I have discovered and nurtured a deep love for web development. The hobby began when I couldn't find a good RSS reader online, and has resulted in a new laptop, a used and abused Github account, and countless hours spent sipping Red Bull while staring at a screen.

    One of the biggest hurdles while learning was frustration that everything I could make has already been made--and much better. To get past this, I set a rule for myself--every app and project must be fully functional and offer one small feature that goes above comparable projects. For example, my technical documentation page has a dark and light mode available, the pomodoro timer settings can be tweaked at any time while it is running, and the calculator displays a running history of your recent calculations.

    On the following page, each project card has 3 pieces of info:

    1. Technologies Used
    2. Extra pizzaz
    3. The hardest part

    Important side notes: First, I have a gluttonous craving for newer and bigger challenges. And second, if you invest in caffeine, you invest in me.

    I am a self-taught developer with a zeal for creating strategic solutions to real-life challenges.
