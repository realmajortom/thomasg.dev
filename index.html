    Welcome, I'm Thomas!

    After shuffling between career paths, I have discovered a deep love for web development. The hobby began when I couldn't find a good RSS reader online, and has resulted in a laptop upgrade, a used and abused Github account, and countless hours spent sipping Red Bull while staring blanky at my screen. Surely I'm not the only one!

    One of the most challenging things as a programmer is the feeling that everything I make has already been done. To get past this, I set a rule for myself--every app and project I develop must be fully functional and do just a little more than comparable projects.
