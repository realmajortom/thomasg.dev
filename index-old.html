    I am a full-stack Web Demolisher from Houston, Texas.

    Besides the fact that it's home to NASA's Mission Control and the Houston Astros, you should also know that Houston isn't part of the west Texas desert--it's a coastal swamp, nestled against the humid gulf.

    As a man of the swamp, trudging through the muck to get stuff done is second nature to me. I love to analyze a problem, break into bite-sized chunks, and implement creative solutions until it's solved. Programming is a perfect fit!
